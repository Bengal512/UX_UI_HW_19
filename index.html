Ben Galang

Hello. I am a designer who loves to do research. I strive to create thoughtful, user centric, and impactful user experiences. I was once a Quality Assurance Analyst for thirteen years, so I know a lot about software testing, how a user thinks, have a creative mindset with the ability to approach a problem creatively. I am easy to work with and have excellent analytical skills. Building upon these skills have helped me become a better User Experience Designer. I look forward to seeing, hearing and talking to you about your project.

My UX Super Power

User Research

I enjoy discovering user's needs, expectations and motivation because I feel that this helps shape the direction of the design and what the users will find pleasing when they are using the application.

Colaboration not Isolation

Some of my best ideas come from great collaboration with my teammates. I have worked with many groups and I have always had an impact on the team because I listen to their ideas and provide constructive feedback on their design thinking with my input and use that towards creating a better solution for our product. I enjoy working with people who I know are better than I am design wise so I am always learning and improving my skills as a UX Designer.
